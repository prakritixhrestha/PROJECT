Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Simply browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will need to create an account or sign in to complete the process.
What are the payment options?
We accept eSewa, Khalti, and Cash on Delivery. All online transactions are processed through secure gateways.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is placed, you can track its status in the "My Orders" section of your profile. We provide real-time updates as your furniture moves through our delivery system.
Do you offer interior consultation?
Yes, we provide professional interior consultation services. Please contact our support team or visit our "Our Services" section for more details.
